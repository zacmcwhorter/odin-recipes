Nutty Pudding

Description

The most delicious naan recipe that came directly from the streets of Mumbai, you will surely love the taste.

Using fresh ingredients that are locally sourced, you will be surprised how easy it is to cook up this divine naan.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 1 pack of yeast
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • 4 tbsp fresh yoghurt

Steps

  1. add packet of yeast to warm water, cover with towel
  2. after 15 minutes, pour liquid mixture into bowl of flour
  3. add olive oil, yoghurt
